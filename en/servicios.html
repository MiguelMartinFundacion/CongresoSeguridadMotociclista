How to arrive

By car

Mobility City is next to the historic city centre and is directly accessible from the Z-30, A-2, Z-40 and AP-68 roadways.
Ample parking facilities are located close to the site, with up to 1,200 spaces available on Avenida de Ranillas and Avenida de Pablo Ruiz Picasso.

By plane

Just a 15-minute drive away is the Zaragoza Airport, offering daily international connections.

By train

Delicias Station (with AVE, Cercanías and Bus Station stops) is only 5 minutes away by car and 15 minutes on foot.

On foot

A 25-minute walk from Plaza del Pilar.

By bike

The Bizi service has several stations very close to the site.

By bus

The site is convenient to a number of bus lines: lines 23 and 34, and at Delicias Station lines Ci1 and Ci2.

By taxi

A taxi rank is located at the north gate on Avenida de Ranillas.
